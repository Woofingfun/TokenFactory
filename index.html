How It Works?

Launch your token on BSC with Jace TokenFactory! It’s fast and secure:

  • Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask to connect to BSC (chain ID: 56). Ensure you have enough BNB for gas fees.
  • Create Your Token: Enter a name (e.g., “JaceCoin”) and symbol (e.g., “JACE”). A premine of 1000 trillion tokens is minted to your wallet.
  • View Your Tokens: Fetch the latest 100 tokens you’ve created, including addresses, names, symbols, and metadata.
  • Explore Latest Tokens: View the latest 100 tokens created by all users on the platform.
  • Secure & Efficient: Built on BSC for low fees and secure creation with metadata support.
